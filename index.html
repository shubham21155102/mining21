A class representative is a student who represents the views of his or her class on academic matters to the lecturer, Department and Faculty. The types of issues a Class Rep would mainly raise are issues that mainly impact on the quality of education, and the ability to study effectively, such as assessment, access to paper reserves, the quality of tutorials, papers, materials teaching facilities.
